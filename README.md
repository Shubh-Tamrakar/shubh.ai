# 🤖 Shubh.AI – Your All-in-One AI Chat Companion

Welcome to **Shubh.AI**, an intelligent LLM-powered chatbot designed to assist you in multiple areas of life — from your career to your finances, and everything in between.

---

## 🌟 What is Shubh.AI?

**Shubh.AI** is more than just a chatbot. It’s your personal mentor, your virtual friend, and your pocket advisor — all rolled into one smart, beautifully designed interface.

Whether you're:
- Navigating your **career path** 🎓
- Exploring **business ideas** 💼
- Looking for **financial advice** 💰
- Stuck in **coding problems** 👨‍💻
- Or just want a **casual conversation** 🤝

**Shubh.AI is here to talk.**  
And yes — **it speaks Hindi too! 🇮🇳**

---

## 🧠 Key Abilities

🔹 **Career Mentor** – Get career guidance, resume help, interview tips, and more.  
🔹 **Business Consultant** – Discuss startup ideas, market trends, or growth strategies.  
🔹 **Finance Adviser** – Manage your money smartly with saving tips, investment insights, etc.  
🔹 **Coding Guide** – Ask your tech doubts and debug with an AI developer buddy.  
🔹 **Friend Chat** – Chill, vent, or just chat — Shubh.AI is always listening.  
🔹 **General Discussion** – From news to philosophy, explore any topic.  

> ⚡ Multilingual Support: English + Hindi  
> 💬 Natural, human-like conversations  
> 🌓 Beautiful dark-themed responsive UI
